Samuel Feenane-Levine


Founder and designer of Periodic Archery.

I'm an olympic recurve archer - currently on the National Junior Squad with 2 National titles - with a love of computer science. I designed this website with the wants of my team-mates in mind. I felt that if we would benefit from this there were surely other clubs, teams and individuals that could use this application to bolster their training. This motivated me to make this free resource for archers all over the world to use.
I wrote the code in this project and brought great people together to make it happen.


Contact: sfeenanelevine@gmail.com


Jim Conroy


Olympic Archer and Coach of Olympic Archers

Jim introduced me to the concept of periodization a few years ago. He explained how he used it during his most competitive time in the sport and how he now uses it with his own students. Jim advised me on plan duration and was a source of constant motivation and insight throughout the project.
